KREUZER-SKARKE REFLEXIVE POLYTOPES DATABASE
============================================

Dataset Name: Kreuzer-Skarke Reflexive Polytopes (Dimension 4)

Description:
Complete classification of 4-dimensional reflexive polytopes used to construct
Calabi-Yau threefold hypersurfaces in toric varieties. This is the largest
public catalog of such objects, containing 473,800,776 reflexive polytopes.

Source:
- Primary Database: TU Wien Calabi-Yau Database
- URL: http://hep.itp.tuwien.ac.at/~kreuzer/CY/
- Alternative: http://quark.itp.tuwien.ac.at/~kreuzer/CY/

Reference Papers:
- M. Kreuzer and H. Skarke, "Complete Classification of Reflexive Polyhedra
  in Four Dimensions", Adv. Theor. Math. Phys. 4 (2000) 1209-1230
  arXiv:hep-th/0002240

- M. Kreuzer and H. Skarke, "PALP: A Package for Analyzing Lattice Polytopes
  with Applications to Toric Geometry", Comput. Phys. Commun. 157 (2004) 87-106
  arXiv:math/0204356
